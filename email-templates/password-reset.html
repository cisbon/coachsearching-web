Creating a Strong Password

When choosing your new password, make sure it:

  • Is at least 8 characters long (12+ recommended)
  • Contains a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Includes numbers and special characters
  • Is unique to this account (don't reuse passwords)
  • Isn't easily guessable (avoid common words, birthdays, etc.)
