# Session Notes & Progress Tracking - User Guide

## ğŸ¯ Overview

The Session Notes feature is a **wizard-based system** designed specifically for coaches who manage 10+ clients. Instead of lengthy note-taking, you quickly capture session insights by clicking through a 5-step wizard with optional text fields.

### Why This Feature?

**Problem:** Coaches with multiple clients struggle to remember individual growth paths and session details.

**Solution:** Quick, structured session capture with minimal typing - mostly button clicks!

## ğŸš€ Quick Start

### For Coaches:

1. **Access Session Notes**
   - Log in as a coach
   - Go to Dashboard
   - Click "Session Notes" tab

2. **Select a Client**
   - Browse your client list
   - Click on a client to view their history
   - Or click "+ Add Session Note" to start fresh

3. **Complete the 5-Step Wizard**
   - **Step 1: Overview** - Client's mood, energy, focus areas (30 seconds)
   - **Step 2: What We Covered** - Topics, achievements, breakthroughs (1 minute)
   - **Step 3: Insights** - Challenges and discoveries (1 minute)
   - **Step 4: Next Steps** - Action items and follow-up (1 minute)
   - **Step 5: Summary** - Ratings and optional notes (1 minute)

4. **Save or Continue Later**
   - Click "Save Draft" to finish later
   - Click "Complete & Save" when done

**Total Time: 4-5 minutes per session**

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### During the Session

1. **Keep It Open**
   - Have the wizard open during your session
   - Fill it in real-time as you go
   - Takes just seconds between topics

2. **Use Voice Notes First**
   - Record quick voice notes during session
   - Transfer to wizard right after

   - Won't lose your progress

3. **Review Before Next Session**
   - Check previous notes before next session

   - Remember what to focus on

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Session Notes" tab not showing
**Solution:**
1. Clear browser cache
2. Make sure you're logged in as a coach
3. Check if session-notes.css is loaded

### Issue: Wizard not opening
**Solution:**
1. Check browser console for errors
2. Ensure sessionNotes.js is imported correctly
3. Verify you have clients with bookings

### Issue: Notes not saving
**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify Supabase connection
3. Check if database schema is installed
4. Look for RLS policy errors in console

### Issue: Can't see client's previous notes
**Solution:**
1. Verify RLS policies are applied
2. Check if you're viewing the correct client
3. Ensure notes exist (check database)
